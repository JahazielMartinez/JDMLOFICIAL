Obra de teatro/Musical - El Arca De Noé Bajo El Mismo Techo

  • Ministerios La Hermosa
  • Dec 24, 2021

Noah's Ark is a play that was inspired by the musical "Noah's Ark - Under the Same Roof (Marcos Vidal 1999)". For 3 generations it was presented live in the Christian Church "La Hermosa - Casa que da vida". During this project we faced different challenges, both logistically and technically. Thanks to technology we have had the opportunity to record this work and have done our best work in post-production editing.

Directing the entire multimedia team in this work was quite a challenge, not all of them had experience in recording, however we managed to have satisfactory results. Thanks to this project I was able to apply my knowledge in different areas. One of them and the most important was leading an entire team together with the general director Eleazar Martínez Peña. It also helped me to apply knowledge of post-production, audiovisual language and live recording.

It was a great adventure in which I was able to learn more about my leadership and was able to work with different areas, understanding that a great job will always have a great team behind it. Despite the adversities we face, I feel grateful for the great opportunity that the Christian church "La Hermosa" gave me for allowing me to direct and put into practice my acquired skills. - Jahaziel Martínez
